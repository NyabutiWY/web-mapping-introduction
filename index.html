Here are web mapping project ideas

Disaster Response and Relief: A web mapping application that assists in disaster response and relief efforts. Using real-time data and satellite imagery to identify affected areas, display evacuation routes, emergency shelters, and available resources. Providing information on road closures, weather conditions, and the location of rescue teams.

Outdoor Recreation Guide: A web mapping application that serves as a guide for outdoor enthusiasts. Incorporating hiking trails, campgrounds, scenic viewpoints, and other points of interest onto a map. Allowing users to plan their outdoor adventures by providing information on trail lengths, difficulty levels, nearby amenities, and user reviews.
